About

A network graph visualization of Hackernews stories and comments!

  • Top - Current top stories (last 15)
  • New - New stories on site(last 100 update 10/s)
  • Best - Best stories of all-time(last 5)
  • Single story
  1. Story posted on HN (Circle)
  2. Comment about a comment
  3. Another story posted by same user (Dotted line)
  4. Comment about the story (Rectangle)
